French stories for use in English schools. The abridg- 
ment préserves ail the essentials of the original taie and 
moves with dramatic interest, but the language, while 
remaining crisp and colloquial, has been adapted where

readers whose knowledgé l>f French is still very limited. 

The book should be‘ ïuitable for beginners in ail
